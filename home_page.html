Mexico City

"The Heart of Mexico"

Mexico City is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant arts scene, and delicious cuisine. Visitors can explore traditional Mexican dishes such as tacos, tamales, and mole poblano.

Mexico City boasts numerous landmarks and attractions, including the historic center, Chapultepec Park, the National Museum of Anthropology, and the iconic Frida Kahlo Museum (Casa Azul).

Mexico City is one of the largest cities in the world, with a population of over 21 million people. It covers an area of approximately 1,485 square kilometers.
